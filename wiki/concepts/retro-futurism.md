# Retro-Futurism

A UI style that imagines the future through a past-era lens — typically 1980s sci-fi. Combines nostalgic analog aesthetics with futuristic content and functionality.

## Visual characteristics

- CRT scan lines and screen curvature effects
- Pixel/monospace fonts (VT323, IBM Plex Mono)
- Neon color palette: pink, purple, green, cyan
- Blinking cursors, terminal-style text rendering
- Grid lines, star fields, analog gauge elements

## Related styles on the spectrum

| Style | Era reference | Mood |
|---|---|---|
| **Retro-Futurism** | 80s sci-fi | Nostalgic wonder |
| **Synthwave** | 80s music/film | Neon-soaked atmosphere |
| **Vaporwave** | 90s internet | Ironic, dreamy, glitched |
| **Cyberpunk** | Near-future dystopia | Gritty, high-tech low-life |

These overlap significantly but differ in mood and cultural reference.
