# Raygun Gothic

The earliest [[retro-futurism]] subgenre, rooted in the 1930s–50s — streamlined chrome, atomic optimism, and art deco geometry projected into space. The future as imagined by someone who loved the Chrysler Building and Flash Gordon serials in equal measure.

## Visual characteristics

- Dark mahogany backgrounds (`#1a0e05`)
- Warm amber (`#f5c842`) as primary, red-orange accent
- Ornate geometric borders, fan shapes, symmetry
- Notched corners via `clip-path`, gold borders
- Fonts: Orbitron for headers, Rajdhani for data labels

## Key references

- Flash Gordon, Buck Rogers
- The Rocketeer
- 1930s–50s World's Fair architecture
- Art Deco meets space age

## Implementation

Uses the Art Deco Tech pattern: ornate geometric borders, warm amber/gold on dark, fan shapes, symmetry. Key components include DecoGauge, ArtDecoCard, FanMeter, OrnamentDivider, ChromeButton, and VoltmeterDisplay.
