# DeepSeek - Wikipedia

Summary of the Wikipedia article for DeepSeek, a Chinese AI company that disrupted the industry in early 2025 with its highly efficient models.

## Key Takeaways

- **Company Profile:** Spun off from the Chinese hedge fund **[[high-flyer|High-Flyer]]** in July 2023. Led by CEO **[[liang-wenfeng|Liang Wenfeng]]**.
- **Efficiency Breakthrough:** Demonstrated that world-class models (like DeepSeek-V3 and R1) can be trained for a fraction of the cost of US counterparts ($6M vs. $100M+).
- **Technical Innovations:**
    - **[[mla-attention|MLA (Multi-head Latent Attention)]]**: Drastically reduces memory and cache usage.
    - **[[mixture-of-experts|DeepSeekMoE]]**: Refined Mixture of Experts with shared and routed experts.
    - **[[grpo|GRPO (Group Relative Policy Optimization)]]**: Reinforcement learning without a separate reward model.
- **Model Strategy:** Uses an **[[open-weight-models|open-weight]]** approach. Significant models include DeepSeek-V3 (efficient SOTA) and DeepSeek-R1 (reasoning).
- **Market Impact:** Described as a **"Sputnik moment"** for the AI industry, proving that massive capital/hardware isn't the only path to progress.

## History & Releases

- **July 2023:** Independent spinoff from High-Flyer.
- **May 2024 (V2):** Introduced MLA.
- **Dec 2024 (V3):** Outperformed established models with low training costs.
- **Jan 2025 (R1):** Reasoning model that reached #1 on US App Store.
- **April 2026 (V4):** Preview of 1.6T parameter model.
